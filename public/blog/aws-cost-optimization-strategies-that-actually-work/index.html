AWS Cost Optimization Strategies That Actually Work

8 July, 2025 | written by Luiz Ferreira

Cloud costs can quickly spiral out of control if left unmanaged. Here are proven strategies to optimize your AWS spending without sacrificing performance.

Right-Sizing Your Resources

The most immediate impact comes from right-sizing your EC2 instances and other compute resources. Many organizations over-provision resources “just in case,” leading to unnecessary costs.

Reserved Instances and Savings Plans

Strategic use of Reserved Instances and Savings Plans can reduce compute costs by up to 75% for predictable workloads.

Storage Optimization

Implementing proper storage lifecycle policies and choosing the right storage classes can significantly reduce your S3 and EBS costs over time.

Automated Cost Management

Using tools like AWS Cost Explorer, Budgets, and third-party solutions helps maintain ongoing visibility and control over your cloud spending.

These strategies have helped organizations reduce their AWS bills by 30-50% while maintaining or improving their infrastructure performance.
